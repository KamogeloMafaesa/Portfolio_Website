ABOUT ME

My name is Kamogelo Mafaesa. I am a recent graduate from the University of the Witwatersrand, I have just completed a Digital Arts degree, I majored in Game design and Interactive media. I am from a small town called Klerksdorp, in the North West province, and that is where I am currently based.

I like creating art, I am always looking for new ways to create art using new technologies. I love learning, and I am always looking to pick up new skills, and I love technology.

My skills include, making games in Unity, making 3D models in blender, making digital illustration, I also draw using code(html and css), logo design, website development. I recently have been looking into ux and ui design, I have been learning about figma and how to create prototypes and I really think that it is something that I want get into.

In terms of web development, I would say that I would be a great fit for the front-end side more than back-end right now; I do hope to become a full-stack developer one day though.
